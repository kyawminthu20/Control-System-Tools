## Tool Pages

<div class="workflow-card-grid">
  <div class="workflow-card">
    <h3><a href="{{ '/tools/templates/' | relative_url }}">Engineering Templates</a></h3>
    <p>Downloadable original templates — design basis, SRS, I/O list, wire schedule, loop sheets, FAT protocol, MOC form, and more. Several are generated by the Python toolkit.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="workflow-card">
    <h3><a href="{{ '/tools/engineering-toolkit/' | relative_url }}">Python Toolkit (cst)</a></h3>
    <p>The open-source package behind the templates: standards-cited calculators (voltage drop, Art. 430, SCCR), panel design pipeline, commissioning generators, PLC utilities, and diagnostics.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="workflow-card">
    <h3><a href="{{ '/tools/manufacturers/' | relative_url }}">Manufacturer Directory</a></h3>
    <p>Curated major-and-notable vendors by category — PLC/PAC, VFDs, servo/motion, SCADA/HMI, and process instrumentation — with selection criteria and honest scope limits.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="workflow-card">
    <h3><a href="{{ '/tools/rag-browser/' | relative_url }}">RAG File Browser</a></h3>
    <p>Browse and read every markdown file in the canonical reference library — standards intelligence, design framework, training modules, commissioning checklists, and crosswalks.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="workflow-card">
    <h3><a href="{{ '/tools/glossary/' | relative_url }}">Glossary</a></h3>
    <p>Cross-linked A–Z glossary of terms and acronyms across Safety, Electrical, Standards Bodies, and Regulatory domains — with standard-page and lifecycle-stage links on each entry.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="workflow-card">
    <h3><a href="{{ '/tools/crosswalks/' | relative_url }}">Crosswalks</a></h3>
    <p>Side-by-side standards overlap tables — NFPA 79 ↔ IEC 60204-1, UL 508A / NEC / NFPA 79, IEC 61511 ↔ IEC 61508, IEC 60079 ↔ NEC Art. 500/505, plus a compare-any-two picker.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="workflow-card">
    <h3><a href="{{ '/tools/reference-hub/' | relative_url }}">Reference Hub</a></h3>
    <p>Quick-reference architecture and motor-system models — 7-Layer Machine Architecture, Universal Safety Architecture, 15-Standard Compliance Stack, and the Motor Selection Comparison Matrix.</p>
  </div>
</div>
