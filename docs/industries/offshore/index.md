## Industry Profile

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Industry** | Offshore oil and gas platforms, FPSO, subsea systems |
| **Markets** | International |
| **Special concern** | Hazardous areas, corrosion, marine class, IEC 61511 for SIS |

## Standards Path

| Category | Standards | Corpus Status |
|----------|-----------|---------------|
| US electrical | NEC (if US-registered facility) | Complete |
| International electrical | IEC 60204-1 | Complete |
| Process safety | IEC 61511 | Planned — limited <span class="badge badge--verify">TO VERIFY</span> |
| Hazardous area | IEC 60079 family | <span class="badge badge--gap">NOT CONFIRMED IN CORPUS</span> |
| Offshore class rules | DNV-ST-0437, NORSOK | Not in corpus |
| Marine standards | IEC 60092 | Not in corpus |

## Key Overlays

**Corrosion-resistant materials:** Salt air and spray require higher-grade stainless and specialized coatings. Equipment specifications beyond IEC 60204-1 apply.

**Hazardous areas:** Much of an offshore platform is a classified (hazardous) area. IEC 60079 family or NEC hazardous location articles apply throughout. **Not confirmed in corpus.**

**Process safety:** SIS/ESD systems on offshore platforms use IEC 61511. **Limited corpus coverage.**
