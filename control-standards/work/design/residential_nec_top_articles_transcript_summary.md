- Article-specific definitions in `.2` sections apply only within that article.

- Listed and labeled equipment must be installed and used according to manufacturer instructions.
- The transcript frames this as one of the most important field-enforcement rules.

### 5. Branch-circuit loading limits for utilization equipment

The transcript highlights two common loading ideas:

- cord-and-plug-connected equipment not fastened in place should not exceed 80% of branch-circuit rating
- fastened-in-place utilization equipment is discussed in the context of the 50% rule when multiple loads share a branch circuit

- Receptacle spacing rules for residential rooms, countertops, islands, peninsulas, and related layouts

### 7. 240.6(A)

- Standard ampere ratings for fuses and inverse-time circuit breakers

### 9. 310.15(B)(16) / 310.16
