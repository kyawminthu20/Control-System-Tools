### 2. AC/DC applicability is being clarified

- Language is added where needed to make clear whether a rule applies to AC, DC, or both.
- The transcript frames this as part of the code catching up with increasing DC use.

### 3. Long paragraphs are being broken into cleaner lists

- Editorial work is pushing toward shorter subsections and more one-rule-per-section structure.
- The claimed benefit is easier use and fewer field arguments.

### 4. Dot-2 and dot-3 sections are being standardized

- `.2` sections are reserved for reconditioning allowances.
- `.3` sections are reserved for listing requirements.
