# IEC 60204-1 — Safety of machinery: Electrical equipment of machines

Corpus module for **IEC 60204-1:2016+AMD1:2021** (Part 1: General requirements).
Paraphrased engineering guidance and clause routing only — the licensed IEC text
and its numeric tables are **not** reproduced here.

## Use notes

- Edition is **6.0 (2016) as amended by AMD1:2021**; there is no 2018 edition.
- Numeric thresholds (voltage tolerance bands, conductor sizing, temperature
  ranges) are deliberately **not stored** — clauses explain the method and point
  to the licensed table. Any placeholder marked *NOT IN LOCAL CORPUS* must be
  transcribed from the licensed text before use.
- The presentation-layer page lives at `docs/standards/machinery/iec-60204-1/`;
  the site never modifies these corpus files.
