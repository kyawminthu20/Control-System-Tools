# NEC 2023 — Article 310 — Conductors for General Wiring

## 0. Scope for panel conductors

Article 310 covers the general requirements for conductors, including their designation, insulations, markings, and **ampacity** (the maximum current a conductor can carry continuously). In industrial control panels, Article 310 is used to size power feeds and branch circuits, ensuring that the heat generated by the current does not damage the wire insulation or surrounding components.

## 1. Ampacity and derating

## 3. Control panel wire sizing logic

To correctly size a wire for a panel branch circuit:

1. **Determine the Load:** Calculate the continuous load (run for 3+ hours) vs. non-continuous load.
2. **Select Insulation Type:** Common panel wire is **MTW** (Machine Tool Wire) or **THHN/THWN-2**.
3. **Apply Correction Factors:** Check the ambient temperature inside the enclosure and the number of wires in the wire duct.
4. **Verify Terminal Rating:** Ensure the wire size fits and is compliant with the  terminal rule.
